Our Services

Pediatric Speech Therapy

The Lauren Ipsum Speech Clinic is a prominent provider of speech therapy for children in North Alabama. Our highly skilled and trained speech-language pathologists have made a huge impact on the lives of our patients. Our SLPs use individualized strategies for each child's specific needs. Our dedication and care for our children has been the foundation of what makes us a trusted provided to our community.

Evaluations and Treatment

Assessments consist of a mixture of standardized/structured clinical observations and testing to evaluate speech and language weaknesses and strengths. An individualized plan of care is created to ensure that children receive what they need to improve their speech and language skills and become successful communicators.

Speech therapy services are available in the following areas:

  • Articulation Disorders

    • Speech disorders that are caused by difficulty with the physical production of certain sounds.

  • Autism Spectrum Disorder

    • A lifelong disability that brings challenges with communication and social skills.

  • Fluency Disorders

    • Speech disorders that are characterized by repetition or prolongation of words, syllables, or sounds.

  • Language Disorders

    • Disorders that affect auditory comprehension and/or expressive language.

  • Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC)

    • Methods of communication other than speech, such as through the use of an electronic device, which can help to give the patient more control and decrease frustration.

  • Feeding Disorders

    • Impaired oral intake that is not age-appropriate and is associated with medical, nutritional, and/or feeding skill.
