Welcome to The LISC!

Pediatric speech-language pathologists help children communicate effectively by supporting the development of speech and language skills. At The Lauren Ipsum Speech Clinic our therapists are trained to evaluate and treat receptive language, expressive language, pragmatic language, articulation, fluency, and feeding. A specialized treatment plan is developed to help each of our patients reach their speech, language, and feeding needs.

Speech Services Provided

The Lauren Ipsum Speech Clinic provides speech therapy services to children in the areas of:

  • articulation
  • phonological and phonemic awareness
  • fluency
  • expressive language
  • receptve language
  • pragmatic/social skills
  • augmentative and alternative communication (AAC)
  • feeding
