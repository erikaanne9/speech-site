Meet the Lauren Ipsum Team

Lauren Ipsum, M.S. CCC-SLP

Lauren is a certified and licensed Speech-Language Pathologist who received her Bachelor of Science in Communication Disorders from Auburn Univeristy and her Master of Science in Speech-Language Pathology from the University of Tennessee. Lauren began her career working in a variety of local elementary schools and in private speech therapy clinics. She opened her own practice in 2019 and has special interest in articulation disorders and early language disorders in the pediatric population.

Dolores Amet, M.S. CCC-SLP

Dolores is a state and nationally certified Speech-Language Pathologist from Mobile, Alabama. She received her Bachelor of Science and Master of Science in Communication Disorders from Auburn University. Since graduating in 2017, Dolores has worked with a variety of ages and diagnoses. Dolores specializes in language delays as well as childhood apraxia of speech.

Connie Sectur, M.S. CCC-SLP

Connie has a Master of Science in Communication Disorders degree from the University of Mississippi. She has over 20 years of experience working with children and adults in a variety of settings. Her areas of expertice include articulation, language, and feeding disorders in the pediatric population. She also has extensive experience in autism, apraxia, aphasia, and dysphagia.
