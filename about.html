Meet our therapists

Lauren Ipsum is a Speech and Language Pathologist with a Bachelor of Arts in Communication Disorders from Auburn Univserity and a Masters of Science in Speech-Language Pathology from the Univeristy of Tennessee. She holds a Certificate of Clinical Competency from the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association (ASHA) and is licensed as a Speech-Language Pathologist by the States of Alabama. Lauren began her career working in a variety of local elementary schools and in private speech therapy clinics. Lauren opened her own practice in 2010. She has 12 years of experience evaluating and treating children with articulation disorders, expressive and receptive language disorders, fluency, and social communication disorder.

Lauren Ipsum is a Speech and Language Pathologist with a Bachelor of Arts in Biological Sciences from the University of Southern California (USC) and a Masters of Science in Communication Disorders from the California State University Northridge (CSUN). She holds a Certificate of Clinical Competency from the American Speech-Language-Hearing Association (ASHA) and is licensed as a Speech-Language Pathologist by the States of California. Lauren began her career working in the San Diego Pediatric Speech Clinic. She went on to complete her internship at the San Deigo City School District and her externship at Cedars Sinai Medical Center. Lauren opened her own practice in 2010. She evaluates and treats children with articulation disorders, expressive and receptive language disorders, fluency, and social communication disorder.
